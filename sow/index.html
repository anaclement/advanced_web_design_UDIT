WEB ANALYSIS

by Ana Clemente Camacho

  • Purpose Talent
  • Wildy Riftian
  • Atoll Digital
  • Gentlerain

The main objective of this assignment was to analyze four outstanding websites. To deepen the exercise, I decided to choose sites that were each completely different from the others. These sites were selected for their diverse approaches, styles, structure, and design choices among others; all with the aim of exploring a wider range of visual identities and gathering more varied insights for future personal projects.

Purpose Talent

PURPOSE TALENT

It's a dynamic, visually coherent website dedicated to connect ethically driven companies with talented professionals.

Visit full site here

Initial Impression

The first impression is one of warmth, clarity and professionalism. The homepage succeeds in communicating the brand’s mission through a clean layout with engaging animations and scroll-triggered transitions. The overall tone is empathetic and human-centered, conveying trustworthiness and inviting the user to further exploration without making it overwhelming.

Web Structure and Content

Purpose Talent has a multi-page structure with dedicated sections for companies, candidates, and services. Navigation along the site is intuitive and consistent, supported by a fixed top menu and smooth scrolling. The information architecture is also well-organized, making content easy to locate. Each section is concise yet informative, using a friendly and professional tone. The storytelling is subtle but effective, particularly in the “About” and “Services” pages, where purpose and impact come across clearly. Overall, the site avoids clutter and uses the graphic elements as a tool to reinforce the brand's values.

Visual Design and Identity

Purpose Talent’s visual identity is strong and cohesive, built around a warm and earthy color palette. The primary colors (brown and beige) convey stability, trust, and approachability, while the secondary accents (yellow, red, and purple) add vibrancy and emotional depth. Typography is modern and sans-serif, enhancing readability and clarity. Visual hierarchy is well-structured, guiding the user through bold headings, animated cards, and layered layouts. In general, as previously mentioned, the design reflects the brand’s values and fosters emotional connection, perfectly aligning with their mission: it feels grounded, human, and intentional.

Interaction and Innovation

The level of interaction is one of the site’s strongest assets. From the moment the user arrives at the homepage, custom microinteractions, scroll-triggered animations, and UX cards create a dynamic and immersive experience. The site features >scrollable sections, animated text, and responsive transitions that adapt to user behavior. These elements are not just decorative but support the narrative and enhance usability. The flow is smooth and well-paced, with transitions that feel natural and engaging. And while the site does not use 3D, it achieves a high level of interactivity through animations and illustrations that simulate depth and dynamism.

Usability and Accesibility

When it comes to usability, the site is fully responsive, adapting seamlessly to both mobile and desktop devices. Navigation is clear, content is easy to find, and the site performs well in terms of visibility of system status, consistency, and user control (according to Nielsen’s heuristics). However, accessibility could be improved in some way. There is limited evidence of ALT text for images, and some areas suffer from color contrast issues, suggesting that while the site is user-friendly, it does not yet meet full WCAG AA compliance standards.

Technical Performance

About performance, it is solid but not exceptional. Though the site loads quickly on most devices, a Lighthouse audit reveals room for improvement. The images are not fully optimized (there is no lazy loading or WebP formats), and semantic HTML is underutilized as many sections rely on generic DIV tags instead of semantic elements like SECTION or ARTICLE. SEO is basic: although the title tag is present, meta descriptions and structured data are missing. Such technical aspects affect both accessibility and search engine visibility and should be addressed to enhance the site’s robustness.

Excelence Criteria

Based on the Awwwards standards we can conclude that in terms of:

✶

Design

The site presents a consistent visual identity, with a warm color palette and a clean, well-structured layout that work as a whole and succeed in conveying the brand values.

○

Usability

Navigation is intuitive and responsive across devices, offering a smooth user experience, although some accessibility improvements could be made.

✶

Creativity

The site stands out for its use of scroll-based storytelling, animated text and illustrations, and microinteractions that create an engaging user flow and experience.

○

Content

The message is clear and aligned with the brand’s purpose, although deeper semantic structure and better SEO would strengthen the site's technical foundation.

Conclusions and Takeaways

Purpose Talent stands out as a well designed, purpose-driven website that balances clarity, aesthetics, and interactivity. It demonstrates how visual coherence and dynamic interaction can elevate a brand’s message and engage users. The main takeaway is that a thoughtful design supported by animation and structure can convey a memorable digital experience. To reach award-winning standards, the site could improve its semantic HTML, accessibility compliance, and SEO optimization. Nonetheless, it serves as a strong example of how design can reflect purpose and build trust.

Wildy Riftian

WILDY RIFTIAN

Wildy Riftian is a visually striking and immersive portfolio website created by a visual artist and designer from Indonesia.

Visit full site here

Initial Impression

The first impression of Wildy Riftian is bold, expressive, and artistic. The homepage immediately showcases the creator as a multifaceted visual artist (illustrator, motion designer, and photographer) through a layered, animated interface it invites the user to further exploration. The tone is personal and creative, with a clear emphasis on embracing experimentation. And in general, the site feels kind of like a digital gallery, where content is shown with a strong narrative and a very distinctive visual identity.

Web Structure and Content

The site follows a multi-page structure, with general sections for featured works, about, and contact. Although these are clearly defined and indicated, they are presented in a way that prioritizes visual storytelling over hierarchy. Navigation is minimal but effective, relying on a fixed top menu and scroll-based transitions. The information architecture is unconventional and innovative yet intuitive, guiding the user through a content that is framed more as a narrative than a catalogue. Each section is visually driven and text is used sparingly, allowing visuals to take the lead. Overall, the structure supports a exploratory and clear user flow.

Visual Design and Identity

Wildy Riftian’s visual identity is bold and experimental. The color palette is dark, with deep blacks and muted tones and also a bright yellow that, as a hole, help to highlight the vibrant visuals of the projects. Typography choices are expressive and varied, combining serif and sans-serif styles. There’s a deliberate tension in the layout in a way that some elements break alignment or overlap intentionally, reinforcing the idea of creative disruption. The overall aesthetic feels handcrafted and personal, using the visual language to position the site as a space for artistic exploration.

Interaction and Innovation

Interaction is central to the site’s experience. Scroll-triggered animations, hover effects, and layered transitions create a sense of movement and depth. The site features scrollable galleries, animated project previews, and responsive layouts that adapt to user behavior. More than just decorative elements, these create a completely personalized and immersive experience. While the site does not use full 3D technologies, it employs pseudo-3D techniques such as parallax and transform effects to simulate that depth and layering. The result is a dynamic and engaging interface that feels alive and responsive.

Usability and Accesibility

While the site is responsive and performs well across devices, its experimental nature introduces some usability challenges. Users unfamiliar with non-linear navigation may find certain interactions less intuitive. According to Nielsen’s heuristics, the site succeeds in consistency and aesthetic design but could improve in terms of feedback and accessibility:

  • the LANG=EN tag is missing in the HTML
  • some background colors don't have sufficient contrast ratio
  • units are defined in pixels instead of em values.

These issues suggest that while the site excels in creativity, it could benefit from stronger compliance with WCAG AA standards.

Technical Performance

Technically, the site performs well but leaves room for optimization. A Lighthouse audit indicates average scores, with potential improvements in image compression, lazy loading, and semantic HTML. The structure relies heavily on DIV elements, and meta descriptions are missing, which affects SEO. URLs are clean, but indexing could be improved. These technical gaps don’t affect the artistic impact but do limit discoverability and long-term performance.

Excelence Criteria

Based on the Awwwards standards we can conclude that in terms of:

✶

Design

The site presents a bold and expressive visual identity, with a dark palette and layered layout that reflect the artist’s creative vision.

○

Usability

Navigation is fluid and responsive, though some experimental elements may challenge accessibility and user control.

✶

Creativity

Wildy Riftian excels in creative direction, using animation, visual tension, and interactive storytelling that elevate the experience.

○

Content

The message is personal and authentic, though technical enhancements in semantic structure and SEO would strengthen its foundation.

Conclusions and Takeaways

Wildy Riftian is a powerful example of how digital design can serve as a canvas for artistic expression. It merges visual storytelling, motion design, and interactive flow to create an immersive portfolio that reflects the artist’s identity and values. While the site prioritizes creativity over convention, it could benefit from improvements in accessibility, semantic HTML, and SEO to reach broader audiences. Ultimately, it demonstrates that web design can be both functional and poetic, offering a space where form and meaning coexist.

Atoll Digital

ATOLL DIGITAL

Atoll Digital is a Montreal-based web agency dedicated to the design and development of high-performance websites.

Visit full site here

Initial Impression

The homepage immediately communicates professionalism and clarity through bold typography, modular layout, grids and minimal, simple design. The tone is direct but approachable, emphasizing results and user experience. The interface feels structured and intentional, inviting users to explore services and case studies with ease.

Web Structure and Content

Atoll Digital uses a multi-page structure, with clear sections for services, work, about, blog, and contact. Navigation is consistent and intuitive, supported by a fixed top menu and smooth scrolling. The information architecture is well-organized, with clear headings and logical flow. And the content segmented into blocks, where each section is concise yet informative. The storytelling is linear and strategic, emphasizing business impact over artistic experimentation. Overall, the structure supports a logical and efficient user journey.

Visual Design and Identity

The site’s visual identity is minimal, modern and professional, built around a monochrome palette with subtle accent colors. Typography is bold and sans-serif, enhancing readability and clarity. Layouts are modular and grid-based, with generous spacing and clean lines. Visual hierarchy is achieved through font weight, the mentioned spacing, and contrast. The design reflects the agency’s values of efficiency, reliability, and strategic thinking. There’s no visual excess or complex animations; instead, the emphasis is on structure and readability.

Interaction and Innovation

Interaction is functional and sober. The site features smooth scrolling, hover effects, scroll-triggered transitions, and animated cards that support usability. However, it avoids advanced animations or experimental layouts, opting instead for clarity and control. Elements like immersive storytelling techniques, or really advanced motion designs are not included all throughout the site. The result is a responsive and polished interface, along with a dynamic but restrained experience that prioritizes usability over novelty.

Usability and Accesibility

Usability is strong. The site is fully responsive and adapts well to mobile and desktop devices. Navigation is intuitive and clear, CTA’s are visible and content is easy to locate. According to Nielsen’s heuristics, the site performs well in terms of consistency, visibility, and user control, and even accessibility:

  • image elements have relevant ALT attributes
  • colors contrast ratios meet WCAG AA standards in most areas

These gaps suggest that while the site is user-friendly, it also mostly comply with accessibility best practices.

Technical Performance

Technically, performance is solid. The site loads quickly and performs well across platforms. Nevertheless, a Lighthouse audit indicates that there could be potential improvements in image compression, lazy loading and semantic structure. The site relies heavily on DIV tags, and meta descriptions are present but minimal, which affects SEO. These technical aspects are secondary to the site’s business goals and user experience but do impact discoverability and long-term performance.

Excelence Criteria

Based on the Awwwards standards we can conclude that in terms of:

✶

Design

The site presents a clean and structured visual identity, with a monochrome palette and modular layout that reflect the agency’s strategic focus.

○

Usability

Navigation is smooth and responsive, though accessibility could be improved with better contrast and semantic structure.

✶

Creativity

Atoll favors clarity over experimentation, using subtle animations and refined layouts to support its business-driven message.

○

Content

The tone is professional and persuasive, though technical enhancements in SEO and accessibility would strengthen its foundation.

Conclusions and Takeaways

Atoll Digital’s website is a well-executed agency platform that prioritizes clarity, professionalism and business relevance. It demonstrates how structured design and persuasive content can build trust and succeed in presenting its value. While it lacks the emotional engagement of more creative platforms, it remains consistent with its positioning. To further enhance its impact, the site could improve accessibility, semantic structure, and SEO. Nonetheless, it stands as a strong model of how digital design can support business-driven services.

Gentlerain

GENTLERAIN

Gentlerain is an AI-powered platform for practicing soft skills through immersive workplace simulations.

Visit full site here

Initial Impression

The homepage of Gentlerain immediately conveys a sense of innovation and immersion. The use of large typography, strong and vivid colors, animated transitions, and layered visuals creates a dynamic, fun first impression. The tone is motivational positioning the user as the protagonist in a journey of professional growth. The interface feels modern and playful, with a clear emphasis on storytelling and user interaction.

Web Structure and Content

Gentlerain adopts a single-page structure with smooth scroll navigation. The layout is divided into thematic sections: product concept, features, user benefits, and business solutions. Each block is visually distinct, using background changes and animations to guide the user’s attention. Navigation is linear but intuitive, with anchor links and CTAs placed strategically. The storytelling is immersive, presenting the platform as a narrative-driven tool. Content is concise and segmented, with a strong focus on benefits and use cases rather than technical details.

Visual Design and Identity

The visual identity is bold, futuristic, and immersive. The color palette combines dark backgrounds with vibrant accent colors, creating contrast and depth. Typography is large, sans-serif, and expressive, reinforcing the motivational tone. Layouts are layered and animated, with overlapping elements and scroll-triggered transitions that add movement. Visual hierarchy is achieved through size, contrast, and animation. The design reflects the brand’s values of innovation and personalization. It avoids minimalism in favor of a more cinematic and engaging aesthetic.

Interaction and Innovation

Interaction is a key strength of Gentlerain. The site features scroll-based animations, hover effects, and dynamic transitions that enhance the immersive experience. The storytelling approach is interactive, with the user “playing” throughout the web. While the site doesn’t include advanced motion design or 3D elements, it does include animation, horizontal carousels and layout shifts to simulate progression and engagement.

Usability and Accesibility

Usability is solid, though accessibility could be improved. The site is responsive and adapts well to different screen sizes. Navigation is clear and CTAs are visible. However, some text-over-background combinations may challenge readability, and contrast ratios could be optimized for WCAG compliance. ALT attributes and semantic structure are not fully evident, suggesting room for improvement. The immersive design may also pose challenges for some users.

Technical Performance

Performance is decent but not exceptional. The site loads quickly, though the use of animations and layered visuals may affect speed on lower-end devices. A technical audit would likely reveal areas for improvement in image optimization, lazy loading, and semantic HTML. SEO is limited, as the content is mostly visual and lacks detailed metadata. These aspects are secondary to the site’s experiential goals but do impact discoverability and long-term performance.

Excelence Criteria

Based on the Awwwards standards we can conclude that in terms of:

✶

Design

Gentlerain presents a bold and immersive visual identity, with layered layouts and expressive typography that reflect its innovative positioning.

○

Usability

Navigation is smooth and responsive, though accessibility could be improved with better contrast and semantic structure.

✶

Creativity

The site embraces storytelling and interaction, using animation and layout shifts to simulate a narrative experience.

○

Content

The tone is motivational and user-centric, though technical enhancements in SEO and accessibility would strengthen its foundation.

Conclusions and Takeaways

Gentlerain’s website serves as an example of how immersive design and storytelling can support skill development and user engagement. It prioritizes experience over technical precision, creating a platform that feels dynamic and fun to use. While it could benefit from improvements in accessibility and semantic structure, its creative approach and clear messaging make it a strong contender in the field of AI-driven training tools. For future iterations, enhancing performance and accessibility would help broaden its reach and impact.
